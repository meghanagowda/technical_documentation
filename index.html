Steps to prepare Chicken Biriyani

Ingredients
Servings: 4
  • 2 lbs chicken drumsticks
  • 1 cup curd
  • 5-6 green chillies
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 100 gms fried onion(Birista)
  • 1/4th tsp tumeric
  • 1 tbsp biriyani masala powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chilly powder
  • 1/2 cup of finely chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup of finely chopped mint leaves
  • whole spices(cassia, cloves, cardamom , bay leaf , stone flower , pepper corns , start anise )
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp ghee(clarified butter)
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 2 cups long grain basmati rice
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tomato finely chopped
  • salt to taste
Marination
This step brings depth of flavors into the dish

In a bowl add in the birista, yogurt, chopped coriander leaves, chopped mint leaves, salt , chopped green chillies , red chili powder, turmeric powder , ginger-garlic paste and garam masala.

Mix the marination well, add in the drumsticks(make some incisions on them for absorbing the marination) and mix well. Cover and let it marinate for 1 hour.

Preparing Flavored Rice
Flavored rice adds more dimension to the dish.

Soak rice for 1 hour. Boil 8 cups of water, add salt to season the water and oil from the birista. add the soaked rice and boil till 75% done.

Drain the rice and spread in a plate.

Biriyani gravy
Preparing the best part.

In a pan add in the oil and ghee. Once hot, cumin seeds and after they sizzle, goes in cardamom pods, cloves, peppercorns, bay leaves and star anise. Add in the chopped onions and cook till they are golden.

Add the chopped tomatoes and salt. Cook till the tomatoes are mushy, then add the marinated mixture and cook until the drumsticks are tender.

Layering
This is where the magic happens.

To begin layering the biryani take heavy bottom pan and grease with clarified butter. Add a layer of rice , sprinkle biriyani masala powder , add some birista and on top of this add the gravy.

Top it with one more layer of rice and birista and some ghee. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes , seal the lid to prevent steam from escaping Open the pan, serve the biriyani.

Tip : You can add cashews in between layers to add extra texture.
